About TendaCare

Our Mission

TendaCare was created to address the growing need for immediate response to community emergencies, with a special focus on Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV) that disproportionately affects women and youth in digital spaces.

We bridge the gap between survivors and support services through technology, ensuring timely intervention while maintaining strict confidentiality and safety protocols.

Simple steps to get help when you need it most

Safe and Confidential

Ongoing Support

Report an Incident

Your safety is our priority. We're here to help.

File a GBV Report

Submit a confidential report of gender-based violence. All information is protected with end-to-end encryption. Choose to remain anonymous or create a secure account for follow-up support.

  • Secure & confidential reporting
  • Option for anonymous reporting
  • Immediate connection to support services

Other Emergency Report

Report other community emergencies including natural disasters, medical emergencies, or safety concerns. Our platform connects you with the appropriate response teams.

  • Fire, flood, medical emergencies
  • Community safety concerns
  • Connection to local authorities
